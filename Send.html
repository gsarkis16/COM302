Send

After the item is packed:

  • Verify the address that you need to send the package to
  • Make a research on which courier or postage companies to use, in particular pay attention to details such as traveling time, prices and legitimacy of the company
  • Most of the postage websites have shipment cost/time calculators, where you will be able to play around with settings of shipment
